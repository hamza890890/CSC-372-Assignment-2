Make your next visit to Japan. With beautiful scenery, delicious food, and a rich culture, you can enjoy a vacation that’s both fun and affordable.

Discover the Tohoku Region

If you're looking for a unique and unforgettable experience, head north to Tohoku. This beautiful area in the northern part of Honshu is full of stunning natural scenery, welcoming locals, and peaceful spots that many tourists miss.

In Aomori, you can experience the vibrant Nebuta Festival, explore the fascinating Nebuta Museum, or take in the views from ASPAM Tower overlooking the bay. Down in Sendai, enjoy a walk along the tree-lined Jozenji-dori Avenue, visit the historical Zuihoden Mausoleum, and take in the sights at the Aoba Castle ruins.

Nature lovers will find plenty to explore. Hike around Lake Towada, follow the trails along Oirase Gorge, or head into the mountains of Zao for hiking in the summer and skiing in the winter. After a long day, unwind in the natural hot springs at Nyuto Onsen or Ginzan Onsen, both surrounded by peaceful forests and cozy traditional inns.

And of course, there's the food. Try crisp Aomori apples, the fun and fast-paced wanko soba from Iwate, or warm up with a bowl of Akita’s famous kiritanpo. Each prefecture in Tohoku offers its own unique dishes that reflect the region’s rich history and traditions.

Whether it’s your first time in Japan or your fifth, there’s always something new to discover. If you want a slower pace, beautiful nature, and authentic culture, the Tohoku region is the perfect place to start.
